About Us

Inks Anonymous is the first tattoo parlor and bar combination, created to make it more fun and relaxing to get inked up.

Services

What We Do.

Come have a drink and get a tattoo at New York's first combination bar and parlor! A classy spin on the sterotypical tattoo parlors, a moder-rustic feel with elegant inserts.

Our Place

We are located in the heart of Soho at 246 Spring Street, New York, NY 10012. We are open from Sunday to Thurday 10:30am - 11:00pm & Friday and Saturday 10:30am - 2:00am.

The Bar

Our renowned bar offers inspired cuisine in an unforgettable setting, artfully crafted with the freshest seasonal ingredients. Discover tempting treats and snacks with our new Bar Bites. Embark on an adventure through our award-winning cellar. We still have bottles for Liz Taylor, Joan Crawford and more. Other historic gems include an 1825 Madeira, bootleg gin from 1919 and even Suntory Whisky gifted to us in 1941 by an admiral in the Japanese Navy.

Private Events

Host a private event with us, and we’ll ensure the cuisine and drinks are every bit as sensational as the venue. From delicious hors d’oeuvres that complement your cocktail reception to tattoos upon your wildest dreasm, we’ll create an event worth writing about. Contact

Tattoos

Inks Anonymous is known as one of the best tattoo shops in the New York area. We are a custom tattoo shop but also have pre-made designs for those who need inspiration. Our tattoo artists are skilled professionals who specialize in designing completely original tattoos for every client. We will do our best to accommodate any idea.
